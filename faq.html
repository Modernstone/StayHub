FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How do I search for hotels in a specific location? To search for hotels in a specific location, simply enter the name of the city, region, or landmark in the search bar on our website's homepage. You can also use advanced search filters to narrow down your options based on preferences such as price range, star rating, and amenities.
What are the check-in and check-out times? The standard check-in time is usually around 3:00 PM, and check-out is typically by 11:00 AM. However, these times may vary slightly depending on the hotel's policies. You can find specific check-in and check-out times listed on the hotel's page during the booking process.
What amenities are included in the room? The amenities included in each room vary depending on the hotel. Common amenities may include free Wi-Fi, complimentary breakfast, air conditioning, toiletries, and more. You can view the list of amenities for each room type on the hotel's page before making a reservation.
Is breakfast included in the room rate? Whether breakfast is included in the room rate depends on the hotel and the specific room type booked. Some hotels offer complimentary breakfast as part of the room rate, while others may charge an additional fee for breakfast. You can check the room details or contact the hotel directly for clarification.
What is the cancellation policy? Our cancellation policy varies depending on the hotel and the type of reservation you make. Generally, we offer a range of cancellation options, including free cancellation up to a certain date or time before check-in, as well as non-refundable rates with stricter cancellation policies. You can find the specific cancellation policy for your reservation listed during the booking process.
